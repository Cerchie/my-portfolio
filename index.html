Hi! I’m a software engineer from Phoenix, Arizona. I enjoy knocking out solutions in JavaScript and Python.

I’m eager to learn. I like putting in the work to find creative solutions under constraints. Of course, software development gives me ample opportunity to persevere and to think critically. I’m also a communicator. In my spare time, I’ve volunteered giving educational presentations at women’s events, and I’ve written articles for Darling Magazine, one of the first magazines that took a stance against retouching models. I was on elementary school staff for the first few years of my career. I love using my voice to teach and to build stronger teams.
